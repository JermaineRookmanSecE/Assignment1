Who Are We?

We are a fan-website for the popular Zombies gamemode inside of the Call of Duty games created specifically by Treyarch. Currently we are an outlet to help fellow players learn how to complete easter eggs for hard-to-play maps like Origins or Gorod Krovi.



This website will also be utilized to publish strategies for optimal gameplay, especially for hitting certain high rounds for specifically maps of the server. If you believe that you have the most optimal strategy for a map, please do contact us with video proof of you pulling off said strategy - See Contact Us.
